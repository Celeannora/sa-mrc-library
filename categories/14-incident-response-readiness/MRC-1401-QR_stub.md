## Procedure Steps

| Step | Action | Expected Result |
|------|--------|----------------|
| 1 | Retrieve current IR Plan. Verify version and date are current (reviewed within last quarter). | Current version confirmed. |
| 2 | Review escalation contact list — verify all POC names, phone numbers, and emails are accurate. | All contacts reachable. |
| 3 | Confirm ISSO, ISSM, and AO notification procedures are documented and understood by the SA team. | Procedures current and understood. |
| 4 | Review any security incidents from the previous quarter. Verify lessons learned are incorporated into the plan. | Lessons learned documented. |
| 5 | Confirm incident ticketing system is operational and test-create a draft ticket. | System operational. |
| 6 | Note any updates required to the IR Plan and submit to ISSM for approval. | Update request submitted if applicable. |
| 7 | Sign and date MRC. | MRC complete. |
