## Procedure Steps

| Step | Action | Expected Result |
|------|--------|----------------|
| 1 | Review GPO / MDM policy settings for USB/removable media restrictions. Confirm enforcement is active. | Policy enforced on all in-scope endpoints. |
| 2 | Review endpoint DLP or device control logs for any removable media events. | All events authorized or zero events. |
| 3 | Identify any attempted unauthorized connections. Document and escalate to ISSO. | No unauthorized connections. |
| 4 | Verify authorized media devices are listed in the media authorization registry. | Registry current. |
| 5 | Sign and date MRC. | MRC complete. |
